FOOD AND DRINK TRIVIA GAME INSTRUCTION
To play the Food & Drink Trivia game, click on the Options Page link, choose your desired game difficulty level (Easy, Medium or Hard mode) and your choosen game mode will begin. You have 20 trivia questions to answer in each of the easy, medium and hard mode of the game. You have 10seconds to choose the correct answer from the four available choices for each question before the screen moves automatically to the next question. Good Luck!
